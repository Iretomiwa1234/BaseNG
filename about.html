About BaseNG

Building Africa’s Technology Future

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with BaseNG.

We focus on early-stage technology companies across Africa, with particular emphasis on fintech, healthtech, agritech, and enterprise software. We look for innovative solutions addressing real market needs with strong founding teams.
Our investments typically range from $100K to $2M for early-stage companies. We also provide follow-on funding for portfolio companies showing strong growth and execution.
Our streamlined process typically takes 4–8 weeks from initial meeting to term sheet. We pride ourselves on being founder-friendly and moving quickly when we see a compelling opportunity.
We offer hands-on operational support, access to our global network of mentors and partners, go-to-market assistance, talent acquisition support, and help with subsequent fundraising rounds.
While our primary focus is Africa, we also consider companies globally that are building solutions for African markets or have significant operations on the continent.
You can submit your pitch through our Partnership page. We review all submissions and aim to respond within 2 weeks. Include your pitch deck, key metrics, and a brief overview of your team and vision.
