Core Areas of Focus

We strategically invest in technology sectors driving digital transformation and creating lasting impact across Africa and beyond.

Telecommunications

We support innovative telecommunications solutions that enhance connectivity, efficiency, and digital access. Our interests include infrastructure technologies, value-added services, and next-generation communication platforms.

We invest in data-driven marketing, customer engagement, and digital growth platforms that enable brands and businesses to scale efficiently and measurably across markets. Our focus is on technologies that deliver actionable insights, optimize performance, and drive sustainable long-term growth.

Payment Services & FinTech
