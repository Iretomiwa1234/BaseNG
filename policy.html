Privacy Policy

At BaseNG, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, apply for funding, or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. This includes:

  • Founder & Application Data: Name, email address, phone number, professional history, and any pitch decks or financial documents submitted during the funding application process.
  • Communication Data: Information provided when you contact us for inquiries, support, or event registration.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, cookies, and similar technical data.

2. How We Use Your Information

BaseNG uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Investment Evaluation: Reviewing funding applications and due diligence.
  • Communication: Updates, newsletters, and event invitations.
  • Operations: Relationship management and internal process improvement.
  • Legal Compliance: Regulatory and AML requirements.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data only when necessary:

  • Service Providers: Cloud, CRM, or legal partners.
  • Co-Investors: Only with explicit consent.
  • Legal Requirements: Court orders or lawful requests.

4. Data Security

We apply strong safeguards to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Industry-standard encryption.
  • Access Control: Limited internal access.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access & Correction
  • Deletion
  • Withdrawal of Consent

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
